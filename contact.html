About TrimTracker

A cleaner way to read the lineup.

TrimTracker is designed to make the Ford F-150 lineup easier to understand. Instead of burying visitors in too many disconnected details, the site organizes trims, engines, comparison tools, and buyer recommendations into a clearer flow.

See trims Open compare tools

What makes the site useful

Each page has its own job. The trim page shows the lineup. The engine page explains the main powertrain personalities. The compare page adds both a table and an interactive slider. The buying guide simplifies the decision for different kinds of truck buyers.

Design direction

The darker palette, orange highlights, stronger contrast, and larger imagery give the site a more aggressive truck-focused style while still keeping the text clean and easy to scan.
