Tremor vs Raptor vs FX4.

Pick a terrain and see which F-150 setup makes the most sense.

Tremor

The best middle ground for real trail ability, better stance, skid plates, and off-road confidence without becoming a full desert-performance truck.

Raptor

The enthusiast choice for high-speed off-road terrain, wide-body styling, performance suspension, and maximum attitude.

FX4 Package

A practical package for drivers who want better off-road confidence on normal trims without paying for Tremor or Raptor.
