General Engine Towing Roles

2.7L EcoBoost

Great for daily driving and lighter trailers. Best for people who care about efficiency and do not tow heavy every week.

5.0L V8

Classic truck feel with strong capability, simple personality, and good fit for buyers who like naturally aspirated power.

3.5L EcoBoost

The go-to heavy towing engine. Strong torque and top capability when properly equipped.

PowerBoost Hybrid

Strong torque plus generator-style utility through Pro Power Onboard. Great for campsites, tools, and job sites.
