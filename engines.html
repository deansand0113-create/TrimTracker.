Powertrain Guide

Choose the engine that matches the mission.

Engines are not just horsepower numbers. The best choice depends on towing, daily driving, budget, sound, efficiency, and how the truck will actually be used.

2.7L EcoBoost V6

Best for daily driving, lighter towing, and buyers who want a strong balance of power and efficiency.

5.0L Ti-VCT V8

Best for classic truck feel, sound, and buyers who want a naturally aspirated engine personality.

3.5L EcoBoost V6

Best for serious towing. This is the engine to consider when capability is the priority.

3.5L PowerBoost Hybrid

Best for torque, onboard power, camping, job sites, and buyers who want capability plus useful technology.

High-Output / Raptor Power

Best for performance off-road driving and buyers who want the most aggressive F-150 personality.
