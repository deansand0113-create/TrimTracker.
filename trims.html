Trim cards

Main trims at a glance

Each card is focused on what the trim feels like to own, not just a random list of specs.

XL

The starting point of the lineup. XL is simple, practical, and aimed at buyers who want capability first and appearance second.

Role: entry-level / work truck
Highlights: straightforward cabin, utility focus
Best for: fleet use, job sites, budget shopping

XLT

XLT adds more style and comfort without losing the everyday usefulness that makes the F-150 popular.

Role: best all-around value
Highlights: better tech, cleaner styling, upgrade potential
Best for: daily drivers who still need truck capability

Lariat

Lariat is where the truck starts feeling clearly upscale. It blends premium touches with strong towing and engine flexibility.

Role: premium daily driver
Highlights: upgraded interior, strong tech mix
Best for: buyers who want comfort without jumping to top pricing

King Ranch

King Ranch stands out with western-inspired design and a luxury feel that is more distinctive than just plain chrome and leather.

Role: themed luxury trim
Highlights: signature styling, rich interior materials
Best for: buyers who want upscale features with more personality

Platinum

Platinum focuses on refined design, modern tech, and the cleanest premium look in the gas-powered lineup.

Role: modern luxury truck
Highlights: premium finishes, upscale appearance
Best for: drivers who want a polished high-end F-150

Limited

Limited is aimed at buyers who want the truck already loaded with premium features and very little left to add.

Role: fully loaded flagship feel
Highlights: top-tier equipment and comfort
Best for: buyers chasing the most complete luxury package

Tremor

Tremor is the bridge between a normal F-150 and a full Raptor. It brings more off-road attitude while staying useful as a normal truck.

Role: off-road package with everyday usability
Highlights: trail-ready stance, unique styling
Best for: weekend off-road use without going full desert racer

Raptor

Raptor is the halo truck of the lineup. It is purpose-built for aggressive off-road performance and visual impact.

Role: high-performance off-road flagship
Highlights: wide stance, specialized suspension, serious presence
Best for: enthusiasts who want the most extreme gas F-150

Simple way to think about the lineup

XL and XLT are the value half of the lineup. Lariat, King Ranch, Platinum, and Limited are the comfort and luxury half. Tremor and Raptor are the specialty off-road choices.
