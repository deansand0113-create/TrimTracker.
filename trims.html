Trim cards

Main trims at a glance

Each card highlights a trim’s role in the lineup and a few key buying points.

XL

Work-truck value trim with practical capability and a straightforward interior.

Engine: 3.3L V6
Tow rating: up to ~8,200 lbs
Best for: budget-focused buyers

XLT

A strong everyday value with better styling, more comfort, and popular package options.

Engine: 2.7L EcoBoost V6
Tow rating: up to ~10,000 lbs
Best for: balanced daily use

Lariat

A major step up in comfort and tech with upscale touches and strong capability.

Engine: 5.0L V8 or EcoBoost options
Tow rating: up to ~12,700 lbs
Best for: premium daily driver

King Ranch

Western luxury styling with premium materials and distinctive trim details.

Engine: 3.5L PowerBoost hybrid
Tow rating: up to ~12,700 lbs
Best for: luxury with personality

Platinum

High-end design, loaded technology, and top-tier comfort features.

Engine: 3.5L EcoBoost / PowerBoost
Tow rating: up to ~12,700 lbs
Best for: modern luxury

Limited

Ultra-premium appearance and features for buyers who want the top of the lineup.

Engine: 3.5L PowerBoost hybrid
Tow rating: up to ~12,300 lbs
Best for: maxed-out comfort
