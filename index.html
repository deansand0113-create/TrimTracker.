Work Ready

XL

The simple, durable entry point: vinyl options, practical tech, and the right fit for fleets, work crews, and budget-minded buyers.

  • Best for: work truck use
  • Strength: value and simplicity
  • Upgrade path: STX/XLT styling and tech
Daily Pick

XLT

The sweet spot for many drivers because it blends affordability, chrome or sport styling, better cabin features, and useful daily comfort.

  • Best for: daily driving
  • Strength: balanced price/features
  • Upgrade path: FX4 and tow packages
Tech + Comfort

Lariat

A premium middle trim with major comfort upgrades, stronger technology, leather-trimmed feel, and a more upscale cabin experience.

  • Best for: refined daily driving
  • Strength: luxury without max price
  • Upgrade path: premium audio and driver assist
Trail Built

Tremor

Designed for people who want real off-road hardware without jumping all the way to the Raptor price and performance category.

  • Best for: hunting, trails, dirt roads
  • Strength: off-road capability
  • Upgrade path: winch-ready and trail tech
Western Luxury

King Ranch

A luxury truck with signature interior styling, comfort features, and a more distinct personality than a standard premium trim.

  • Best for: luxury with character
  • Strength: interior design
  • Upgrade path: towing and hybrid power
Executive

Platinum

A high-end street-focused trim aimed at buyers who want comfort, technology, towing confidence, and a polished daily-driving experience.

  • Best for: premium commuting
  • Strength: comfort and technology
  • Upgrade path: PowerBoost hybrid
Desert Monster

Raptor

The performance halo truck with wide-body styling, serious suspension, aggressive stance, and a completely different mission than normal trims.

  • Best for: high-speed off-road
  • Strength: suspension and performance
  • Upgrade path: Raptor R power
